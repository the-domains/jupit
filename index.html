Back in March, one of our writers wrote about her ideal spiritual trip to Israel. In a serendipitous way an opportunity to visit Israel on a spiritual energy tour opened up for us this May. We hope this post will reach your heart and/or soul, rather than your intellect, as we cover the purpose of such a trip and give you the highlights.
